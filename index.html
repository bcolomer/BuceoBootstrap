AbySub Diving School

Welcome to AbySub, a diving school located in Fuengirola. We offer you the opportunity to explore the underwater world in a safe and enjoyable way. In a comfortable and relaxed environment, a certified PADI instructor will guide you through the best diving spots in Málaga, the Costa del Sol and Andalucía,.
